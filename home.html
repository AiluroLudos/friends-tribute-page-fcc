The greatest show in the world


Friends was an American television series that ran from September 22, 1994 to May 6, 2004. The show ran a total of 10 seasons and 236 episodes

The cast

  • Jennifer Aniston
  • Courtney Cox
  • Lisa Kudrow
  • David Schwimmer
  • Matt LeBlanc
  • Matthew Perry

Noteable guest stars

Over the course of 10 years, dozens of high profile actors and musicians made guest appearances on the show, much to audience delight. These included:

  • Brad Pitt
  • George Clooney
  • Julia Roberts
  • Bruce Willis
  • Reece Witherspoon
